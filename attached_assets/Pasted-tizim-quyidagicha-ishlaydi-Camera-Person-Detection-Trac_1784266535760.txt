IDENTITY CONSISTENCY POLICY

The system must never create a new identity simply because the face is temporarily unavailable.

If the face is hidden, turned away, partially occluded or outside the camera view, the system must continue tracking the same person using Person Re-Identification, appearance features and movement continuity.

When a recognizable face becomes available later, it must be associated with the existing persistent identity instead of creating a new profile.

The platform must maintain one persistent identity across all cameras and all observation sessions unless there is strong evidence that the observed individual is different.
