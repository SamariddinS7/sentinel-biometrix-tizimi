Bu bo'lim AI Copilot'ni faqat javob beradigan AI emas, balki butun AI VMS platformasini boshqaruvchi Operations Copilot ga aylantiradi.

====================================================
OPERATIONS CONTROL ENGINE
====================================================

The Enterprise AI Copilot must control the Enterprise AI Video Management System through official platform APIs.
