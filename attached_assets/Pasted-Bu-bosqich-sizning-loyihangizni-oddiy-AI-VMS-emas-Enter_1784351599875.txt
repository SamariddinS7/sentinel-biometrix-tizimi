Sizning Area Map modulingiz uchun quyidagi qoidani albatta qo'shing:

PHYSICAL VISIBILITY ENGINE

The Digital Twin must calculate real camera visibility.

Walls, floors, ceilings, columns, closed doors and structural objects must block the camera field of view.

Only geometrically visible areas may be considered covered.

Coverage visualization must be generated using real ray casting and visibility calculations.

Areas hidden behind walls must never appear inside the camera coverage polygon.

Coverage must update automatically when:

Camera position changes

Camera rotation changes

Camera tilt changes

Field of view changes

Building geometry changes

The visibility engine must become the single source of truth for camera coverage calculations throughout the system.

Bu qoida bilan sizning Digital Twin kameraning ko'rish hududini haqiqiy geometriya asosida hisoblaydi va devor ortidagi hududni "ko'rinadigan" deb chizmaydi. Bu professional VMS va Digital Twin tizimlari uchun muhim talab hisoblanadi.
