SECTION 29 — AUTONOMOUS INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
====================================================
AUTONOMOUS INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
====================================================

The Enterprise AI Copilot operates as an autonomous decision-support platform.

It continuously observes platform state.

It proactively detects operational situations.

It recommends actions.

It executes only authorized actions.

Every recommendation must be evidence-based.

Every action must be auditable.

====================================================

AUTONOMOUS LOOP

====================================================

↓

Analyze

↓

Reason

↓

Predict

↓

Plan

↓

Recommend

↓

Execute (Authorized Only)

↓

Verify

↓

Learn

====================================================

The Copilot must never fabricate observations.

The Copilot must never bypass authorization.

The Copilot must never hide uncertainty.
