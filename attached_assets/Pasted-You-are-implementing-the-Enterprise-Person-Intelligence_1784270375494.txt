INVESTIGATION CONSISTENCY POLICY

The Investigation Platform must never infer facts that are not supported by recorded evidence.

Every timeline entry, movement path, co-occurrence, association, incident and report must reference actual observations recorded by the system.

Whenever confidence is insufficient, the platform must present the result as an observation or possible correlation rather than a confirmed fact.

Every investigation result must include links to the supporting evidence (video clips, snapshots, AI metadata, timestamps and camera identifiers) to allow operator verification.
